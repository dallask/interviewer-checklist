### Integration Warnings

**WARNING 1 — UI-17, BANK-01: SectionFilter doesn't show user-added sections**

`SectionFilter.tsx` iterates `DEFAULT_SECTIONS.map(...)` for button rendering and computes counts from `DEFAULT_SECTIONS` only. A user who adds a section via Phase 14 `addSection` cannot filter to that section via SectionFilter, and the total count is wrong once the bank is mutated.

Impact: The Phase 13 filter promise ("select all of [a section] with a single click") is only partially fulfilled for the Phase 14 use case of user-added sections.

**WARNING 2 — UI-16: DifficultyFilter counts are static**

`DifficultyFilter.tsx` uses `useMemo([], [])` — an empty dependency array. Difficulty counts from user-added questions never appear in the filter counts.

Note: The Phase 13 CONTEXT.md D-02 explicitly acknowledges this and says "useMemo dep array should be updated to [customQuestions] in Phase 14." Phase 14 completed without addressing this.
