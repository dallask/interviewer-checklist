Privacy Policy

Interviewer Checklist — Last updated: June 2026

Data storage

All data entered in Interviewer Checklist (candidate names, scores, notes, and session history) is stored exclusively in chrome.storage.local on your device. No data is transmitted to any server.

No tracking

The extension collects no analytics, telemetry, or usage data of any kind. It makes no network requests.

Deleting your data

To delete all stored data: open Chrome, go to chrome://extensions, click "Details" on Interviewer Checklist, then "Clear site data" — or use the Reset button inside the extension.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue at github.com/dallask/interviewer-checklist.
